LinkedIn Easy Apply by Job type

LinkedIn is the premier digital networking platform utilized by job-seekers and companies to advertise and connect. LinkedIn has a feature called ‘Easy Apply’ that allows a person to apply to a job through their platform by uploading your resume and sometimes answering a few brief questions.
From our analysis, 5% of both Data Analyst and Data Engineer were able to be applied for using LinkedIn’s Easy Apply. Conversely, only 3% of Data Scientist jobs were able to be applied for using LinkedIn’s Easy Apply.
While this is surprising that company’s choose not to use this seemingly convenient feature, this shouldn’t be discouraging! There are still over 380,000 Data Analyst jobs, 700,000 Data Engineer jobs, and 370,000 Data Scientist jobs that can be applied for using LinkedIn’s Easy Apply feature.
